# Superstore Profit Dashboard

## Overview
Analyzed retail sales data from 2014–2017 using Excel to identify trends in profitability, regional performance, and seasonality.

- Source: Sample Superstore.xls
- https://trailhead.salesforce.com/trailblazer-community/feed/0D5KX00000kQPXv0AO

## Key Insights
- West and East regions lead in profitability
- Technology drives the majority of profit
- Profit peaks in Q4 each year

## Business Recommendations
From 2014 to 2017, the West and East regions were the top-performing regions by total profit, with the West leading in every year except 2016. Technology products were the primary driver of overall profitability across all regions. In contrast, Furniture performance was inconsistent, with the Central region experiencing negative average profit per order, indicating potential inefficiencies in pricing or cost structure.
Profitability followed clear seasonal trends, peaking in Q4 each year and declining in Q1 and Q2, suggesting strong end-of-year demand cycles. These findings highlight an opportunity to prioritize Technology inventory and marketing efforts while reevaluating Furniture strategy in underperforming regions.
